Who I am

About Me

I'm a PMP-certified Project Manager with a track record of delivering Agile software projects in fast-moving fintech and banking environments. Over the past several years, I've led cross-functional teams through the full delivery lifecycle — from backlog grooming and sprint planning to stakeholder reporting and post-release retrospectives.

What I enjoy most is turning complexity into clarity: taking a sprawling initiative, breaking it into manageable sprints, and making sure everyone on the team knows what's happening and why it matters. I thrive in environments where the pace is high and the stakes are real.

Beyond software delivery, I've led company-wide compliance initiatives, including guiding an organization to ISO 27001 certification on the first attempt — a project that required aligning 20+ policies and coordinating across every department under a nine-month deadline.
